Why Shiftrix?

Real-Time Shift Tracking

Shiftrix provides real-time tracking of employee shifts, ensuring that managers and employees can instantly view any shift changes, updates, or attendance. Employees can clock in and out directly through the app, with accurate timestamps automatically logged for payroll and record-keeping purposes.

Automated Shift Scheduling

With Shiftrix, managers can create and manage shift schedules in minutes. The platform automatically generates optimized schedules based on employee availability, preferred working hours, and any specific requirements, reducing manual scheduling time and ensuring fairness in shift distribution.

Shift Swapping & Request Management

Employees can request shift swaps with their colleagues through the platform, and managers can approve or deny these requests with ease. This feature enhances flexibility for employees and reduces scheduling conflicts, fostering a better work-life balance.

Compliance & Labor Law Monitoring

Shiftrix ensures that all shifts comply with local labor laws, including maximum working hours, overtime, and mandatory break periods. The platform sends notifications to managers if a schedule violates any rules, minimizing the risk of non-compliance and legal issues.

Customizable Reporting & Analytics

Shiftrix offers powerful reporting tools that give managers insights into employee attendance, shift patterns, labor costs, and productivity. These customizable reports help businesses make data-driven decisions to improve efficiency, streamline operations, and reduce costs.

Mobile App Integration

Shiftrix comes with a user-friendly mobile app, allowing employees to track their shifts, clock in/out, request time off, or view upcoming schedules from anywhere. This mobile integration ensures flexibility for remote or on-the-go workers, improving overall engagement and convenience.
