Features.

Let's take a look into some of the great features that will be launching with Shiftrix. Whilst this isn't a comprhensive list of everything Shiftrix can do, this is what the product will launch once we have the interest and backing!

GPS & Geofencing

Ensure employees clock in from approved locations with GPS tracking and geofencing, reducing time fraud and ensuring compliance for remote or field-based teams.

Automated Overtime & Compliance Alerts

Set up rules to automatically calculate overtime, prevent early/late clock-ins, and send alerts when employees approach labor law limits.

Seamless API Integrations

Connect effortlessly with payroll, HR, project management, and other business tools using robust API integrations, enabling smooth data flow and automation across platforms.

Custom Reporting & Analytics

Shiftrix offers powerful reporting tools that give managers insights into employee attendance, shift patterns, labor costs, and productivity. These customizable reports help businesses make data-driven decisions to improve efficiency, streamline operations, and reduce costs.

Dedicated Helpdesk & Customer Support

Exceptional support with a built-in helpdesk featuring live chat, a ticketing system, and a knowledge base to assist users quickly and efficiently.

Shift Opt-In & Coverage with Advanced Notice

Empower employees to volunteer for open shifts with a customizable notice period, making shift coverage seamless and reducing last-minute scheduling issues.
